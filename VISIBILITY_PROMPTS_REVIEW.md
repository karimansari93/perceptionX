# Visibility Prompts That Will Be Fixed

When a job function is added, these visibility prompts will be updated to remove industry context and focus on the job function:
**"What companies in {industry} are known for X?"** → **"What companies are known for X for {jobFunction}?"**

## Base Visibility Prompt
1. **General**: "What is the best company to work for in the {industry} industry?"
   → Will become: "What is the best company to work for for {jobFunction}?"
